COVID-19 Information

The adoption cetner is currently closed to general public. We are currently operating adoptions through an appointment based system. Contact us via email or phone to schedule an appointment today.

To prevent the spread of COVID-19, we require all staff, volunteers, and clients to please follow the state and CDC guidelines for personal health and hygiene:

  • Face coverings are required inside of all facilities, regardless of vaccination status.
  • All visitors are asked to follow the most current guidelines on social distancing.
  • Frequency of cleaning and disinfecting has been increased in areas of our buildings visited by the public.
  • Hand sanitizer will be made available for use for all visitors and staff.

Shelter Veterinary Services

Every animal that comes to an ARL shelter receives a medical evaluation from one of our highly-qualified, caring shelter veterinarians. Our team members continuously train and work to expand skills to meet the needs of a diverse range of species coming from a variety of living situations and conditions.

The folllowing is included in your new pet's adoption fee:

  • Spay or neuter services
  • Health screening and veterinary examinations
  • Vaccinations
  • Microchip identification and registration
  • Flea, tick, and mite treatment
